News with bias checker

The application will aggregate news and information in real-time from sources requested by a user, categorize them based on content, and then sort that as per individual usage using an ML-based recommendation system Natural Language processing to analyzed if the document is biased towards or against any certain in person.
Media inarguably has the biggest influence over an individual’s life, be it stuff as small as what to cook today, or what to watch on television, to as big as who to vote for in elections. For users, this tool will tell them if the article or the news source they’re following is biased, and if yes, how much. This will make users conscious about how much biased media do they consume and how it affects their daily decisions. System will also analyze other details like who wrote the news, or in research related news who funded the study, and minute details like these to decide in how many ways the information user is going to consume is contaminated
